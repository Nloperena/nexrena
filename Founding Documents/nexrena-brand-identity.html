06 — Brand Rules
Do's & Don'ts
Always
✦
Write the name as Nexrena — capital N only, one word
✦
Use Cormorant Garamond for all headlines and the wordmark
✦
Lead with results and specifics in all client communications
✦
Maintain the dark/gold premium palette across all materials
✦
Use "we" and "Nexrena" — never a founder's name publicly
✦
Keep generous white space — the brand breathes
Never
—
Don't write NEXRENA, nexrena, or Nex Rena
—
Don't use bright or saturated colors outside the palette
—
Don't associate the agency with any individual founder publicly
—
Don't use stock photos of smiling people in suits
—
Don't place the logo on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
—
Don't use playful or rounded fonts — they break the tone
