# Nexrena Business Playbook
Last updated: March 2026

## Positioning

Nexrena is a digital growth agency. We build the digital engine — websites, web apps, and SEO systems — so clients can focus on running their business. We're an infrastructure partner, not a traditional agency. We believe a website should be an engineered system designed to capture leads, accelerate sales cycles, and compound in value over time.

**Elevator pitch:** "We build growth infrastructure for B2B companies — websites, web apps, and search systems that generate revenue and compound over time. One partner, not four vendors."

**Tagline:** "We build the digital engine. You run the business."

## Ideal Client Profile

- **Industry:** Manufacturing (B2B industrial), E-commerce (DTC and B2B), Professional Services (law, consulting, accounting)
- **Size:** SMB to mid-market, typically founder-led or small leadership teams
- **Pain:** Outdated website that doesn't convert, fragmented vendor relationships, weak organic visibility, scaling issues, slow page loads, mobile-unfriendly
- **Budget:** $10K–$50K+ per project
- **Location:** Primarily US-based, but not limited to geography
- **Best-fit signal:** Bad website + growth ambitions + willingness to invest in infrastructure

### Pricing Strategy

- **Position:** Mid-market to low-premium. High-quality alternative to cheap freelancers and bloated agencies.
- **Default:** Quote project-based, not hourly. Default to mid-market rates.
- **Premium triggers:** Client has clear budget, project generates revenue directly, funded company, rushed timeline (+20-30%), complex integrations.
- **Discount triggers:** Startup with genuine constraints + high potential, great referral, strong portfolio piece, long-term retainer potential.
- **Always:** Include a maintenance retainer upsell in every proposal.
- **Floor:** Never price below market minimums. If budget is too low, suggest alternatives (templates, DIY) rather than discounting.
- **Full benchmarks:** See `reference/COMPETITIVE_AND_PRICING.md`

## Sales Process

1. **Lead identified** — inbound (website form, referral) or outbound (research + cold outreach)
2. **Discovery call** — 15–30 min via [cal.com/nexrena/discovery](https://cal.com/nexrena/discovery)
3. **Proposal sent** — within 48 hours of discovery call
4. **Negotiation** — if needed, adjust scope/timeline/pricing
5. **Contract + deposit** — standard contract, deposit before work begins
6. **Kick off** — onboarding checklist, project brief, credentials collected

**Payment terms:** NET 15. Payment via ACH, wire transfer, or Stripe. 1.5% monthly late fee on overdue balances.

## Process

1. **Discovery** — Audit existing digital presence, research competitors, map gaps between current state and buyer expectations
2. **Strategy** — Clear roadmap: what we're building, why, and exactly what success looks like before a single pixel moves
3. **Execution** — Build fast, iterate in the open. Client sees progress weekly. No black boxes.
4. **Delivery** — Launch is the beginning, not the end. Measure, optimize, compound from day one.

## Values / Differentiators

1. **Infrastructure Over Aesthetics** — Pretty sites that don't convert are expensive art. We build systems that generate revenue — and happen to look exceptional.
2. **Radical Transparency** — No black boxes. Client sees progress weekly, understands every decision, owns every deliverable. No lock-in.
3. **Compounding Returns** — Every sprint makes the next one more valuable. SEO compounds. Design systems scale. Analytics sharpen targeting.
