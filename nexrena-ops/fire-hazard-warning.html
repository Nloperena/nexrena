Observed Condition

During on-site service, the equipment area was observed to have poor ventilation and significant heat buildup. Extension cords / standard power strip usage inside the wall box was present while multiple power adapters and high-load game consoles (Xbox and PlayStation) were operating in the same enclosed space.

Fire Hazard Advisory: This configuration presents a current fire-risk condition and is not recommended for continued use without corrective action.
Immediate Recommendations
  • Replace failed low-voltage AV components (example: HDMI splitter) only as needed to restore temporary functionality.
  • Route only low-voltage signal cabling (HDMI / Ethernet) through proper wall pathway / wall box.
  • Remove extension-cord / standard power-strip use from the enclosed wall cavity.
  • Correct AC/high-voltage power handling to a code-compliant setup and improve airflow.
Scope Limitation

Nexrena / servicing technician provided low-voltage AV diagnosis and temporary functionality restoration only. This document does not certify electrical code compliance. Any in-wall AC/high-voltage correction should be performed by a licensed electrician in accordance with local code.
