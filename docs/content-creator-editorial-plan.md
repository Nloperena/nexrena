## 4. Brand Voice Guide (Extension of Founding Docs)

**Tone:** Confident, precise, calm. No hype, no buzzwords.  
**Person:** "We" and "Nexrena" — never "I" or founder names.  
**Claims:** Every claim backed by a specific result or process.  
**Avoid:** "Crushing it," "synergy," "full-stack growth hacks," discounts, urgency tactics.  
**Do:** Lead with outcomes; use concrete numbers; keep generous white space in copy.

**Content-specific:**
- Blog: Educational, actionable. Headlines that promise a clear takeaway.
- Industries: Empathetic to vertical pain; solution-focused.
- Resources index: Curated, not overwhelming. Quality over quantity.
