Before you redesign, audit. Capture what you have. Otherwise you won't know what you lost — or what to preserve.

## Traffic

- **Top pages** — By traffic. What drives the most visits?
- **Channels** — Organic, direct, referral. Which matters?
- **Trends** — Up or down? Seasonal? Context for post-launch comparison.

## Rankings

- **Export** — Top keywords, positions. From Search Console or your tool.
- **Commercial terms** — Keywords that drive leads. Protect these.
- **URL mapping** — Which URLs rank for what? Redirect strategy depends on this.

## Backlinks

- **Top pages** — Which pages have the most links? Don't lose them.
- **Redirect map** — Every URL with backlinks needs a 301 to the new equivalent.

## Technical

- **Indexed pages** — How many? What's in the index?
- **Crawl errors** — Fix before migration. Don't carry debt forward.
- **Core Web Vitals** — Baseline. Compare after launch.
