FAQ pages can capture featured snippets and long-tail traffic. "How long does X take?" "What does Y cost?" — if you answer it, you can rank for it.

## FAQ Schema

- **FAQPage** — Tells Google these are questions and answers. Can trigger FAQ rich results.
- **One schema per page** — Don't stuff 50 FAQs. Group related questions.
- **Accurate** — Schema must match visible content. No hidden FAQs.

## Content Structure

- **Question as H2 or H3** — Clear, natural language. How people search.
- **Answer** — Concise. 2–4 sentences. Direct answer first.
- **Expand if needed** — But lead with the answer. Snippet-friendly.

## What to Include

- **Process questions** — "How long does a website redesign take?" "What's the process?"
- **Pricing questions** — "How much does SEO cost?" "What's the typical budget?"
- **Comparison questions** — "Agency vs. in-house?" "WordPress vs. headless?"
