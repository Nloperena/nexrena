Images are often the biggest performance drain. They're also an SEO opportunity — alt text, file names, and structured data. Optimize both.

## Format and Size

- **WebP** — Modern format. 25–35% smaller than JPEG. Use with JPEG fallback for older browsers.
- **Dimensions** — Serve the size you need. Don't load 2000px for a 400px display.
- **Compression** — Quality 80–85 often looks fine. Test.

## Lazy Loading

- **Below the fold** — Don't load images until they're near the viewport.
- **Native lazy loading** — `loading="lazy"` on img. Supported everywhere.
- **Above the fold** — Don't lazy load. Hurts LCP.

## SEO

- **Alt text** — Describe the image. "Industrial adhesive application on aluminum surface." Not "image1.jpg."
- **File names** — Descriptive. "epoxy-adhesive-spec-sheet.jpg" not "IMG_4823.jpg."
