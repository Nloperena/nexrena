Manufacturers often serve regional markets. Local SEO helps you show up when buyers search "industrial adhesive suppliers Florida" or "B2B manufacturer Orlando." It's not just a Google Business Profile.

## NAP Consistency

- **Name, Address, Phone** — Same everywhere: website, GMB, directories, citations.
- **Format** — Pick one format (e.g., "St." vs "Street") and stick to it.
- **Schema** — LocalBusiness or Organization with address. Helps search engines verify.

## Location Pages

- **If you serve multiple regions** — Dedicated pages: "Serving Florida," "Serving the Southeast." Unique content, not thin placeholders.
- **If you're single-location** — About, Contact, and service pages should mention location naturally.

## Technical Foundations

- **Site speed** — Local pack and organic both favor fast sites.
- **Mobile** — Local searches happen on mobile. Your site must perform.
- **Structured data** — LocalBusiness with address, hours, service area.
