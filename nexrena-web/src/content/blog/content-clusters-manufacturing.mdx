Topic clusters organize content around buyer intent. For manufacturing, that means technical and commercial queries — specs, applications, suppliers.

## The Pillar

- **Broad topic** — "Industrial adhesives," "SEO for manufacturers," "B2B manufacturing website."
- **Comprehensive page** — Overview, key subtopics, links to cluster content.
- **Commercial CTA** — Links to products, services, or contact.

## The Cluster

- **Specific articles** — "Epoxy vs. polyurethane adhesive," "How to choose adhesive for aluminum."
- **Support the pillar** — Each piece covers one subtopic in depth.
- **Internal links** — Cluster → pillar → commercial. Authority flows.

## For Manufacturing Specifically

- **Application-based** — "Adhesives for construction," "Sealants for automotive."
- **Technical** — Spec comparisons, certification guides, material compatibility.
- **Commercial** — "Industrial adhesive suppliers," "B2B adhesive manufacturers."

Build 3–5 cluster pieces per pillar. Quality over volume. Update annually.
