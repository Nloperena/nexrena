A good brief produces good content. A vague brief produces vague content. Here's what to include.

## Keyword and Intent

- **Primary keyword** — The term the page targets.
- **Secondary keywords** — Supporting terms. Natural inclusion.
- **Intent** — Informational, commercial, transactional. Match the content.

## Structure

- **Headings** — H2/H3 outline. What sections, in what order.
- **Word count** — Target range. Not a hard rule, but a guide.
- **Format** — List, comparison, FAQ. Match the query type.

## Internal Links

- **Link to** — Which pages should this link to? Pillar, commercial, related.
- **Anchor text** — Suggested anchors. Keyword-rich, natural.
- **Link from** — Which existing pages should link here? Pass authority.

## Sources and References

- **Data** — Where to find stats. Original research or cited sources.
- **Competitors** — What's ranking? What's missing? Brief should address gaps.
