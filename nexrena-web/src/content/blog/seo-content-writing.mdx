SEO content shouldn't read like a keyword list. It should read like a human wrote it — for humans. Search engines have gotten better at rewarding that.

## Keyword Placement

- **Title** — Primary keyword. Near the front.
- **First paragraph** — Include it early. Natural. Don't force.
- **Headings** — H2/H3 with variations. "How to" "What is" "Why" — match query intent.
- **Throughout** — Naturally. Use synonyms. Don't repeat the same phrase 10 times.

## Avoid

- **Keyword stuffing** — "SEO for manufacturers" 10 times in 500 words. Looks spammy.
- **Unnatural phrasing** — "Best SEO for manufacturers Florida" in the middle of a sentence. Awkward.
- **Thin content** — 200 words with keywords. No value. No rank.

## Structure

- **Answer first** — Direct answer in the first 100 words. Snippet-friendly.
- **Scannable** — Headlines, bullets, short paragraphs. Mobile users skim.
- **Internal links** — To pillar, to commercial pages. Pass authority.
