Consulting sells expertise. Your site needs to demonstrate it — and make it easy for qualified leads to reach you.

## Thought Leadership

- **Insights and perspectives** — Blog, guides, frameworks. Show you understand the space.
- **Original research** — Surveys, reports. Differentiates you from firms that only republish.
- **Speaking and media** — If you have it, showcase it. Third-party validation.

## Case Study Structure

- **Client (if allowed)** — "Fortune 500 retailer" or "Global manufacturer." Anonymize if needed.
- **Challenge** — What was broken? What did they need?
- **Approach** — What did you do? Methodology matters.
- **Outcome** — Numbers. "Reduced cost by 30%." "Cut implementation time in half."

## Conversion Path

- **Consultation** — Primary CTA. "Schedule a call." "Request a proposal."
- **Content offers** — "Download our guide to X." Capture leads earlier in the journey.
- **Clear next step** — Every page. No dead ends.
