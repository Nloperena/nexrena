Cart abandonment is high. 70%+ in many categories. Some of that's inevitable — window shopping, price comparison. But a lot is fixable. Checkout friction loses sales.

## Form Fields

- **Minimal** — Only what you need. Email, shipping, payment. Cut the rest.
- **Single column** — Easier to scan. Faster to complete.
- **Auto-complete** — Address, card. Browsers help. Let them.
- **Progress indicator** — "Step 2 of 3." Reduces anxiety.

## Trust

- **Security badges** — SSL, payment logos. Visible at checkout.
- **Guest checkout** — Don't force account creation. Offer it. Convert first, capture later.
- **Clear return policy** — Link visible. Reduces "what if I need to return?" anxiety.

## Mobile

- **Large inputs** — Easy to tap. No tiny dropdowns.
- **Sticky CTA** — "Place order" always visible. No hunting.
- **Fast** — Every second of delay costs conversions. Optimize.
