Industrial buyers search differently. They don't search "best adhesive." They search "epoxy for aluminum bonding" or "FDA compliant sealant suppliers." Keyword research for industrial SEO needs to reflect that.

## Spec-Driven Queries

- **Materials** — "Stainless steel adhesive," "high-temperature epoxy."
- **Standards** — "ASTM compliant," "ISO certified manufacturer."
- **Applications** — "Adhesive for aluminum to steel," "sealant for concrete."

## Commercial Queries

- **Suppliers** — "Industrial adhesive suppliers," "B2B adhesive manufacturers."
- **Location** — "Adhesive suppliers Florida," "manufacturer Orlando."
- **Volume** — "Bulk adhesive," "wholesale sealants."

## Problem-Solution

- **Fix** — "Fix concrete spalling," "bond dissimilar materials."
- **Guide** — "How to choose industrial adhesive," "adhesive selection guide."

Target these. Generic "adhesive" or "sealant" is too broad and competitive.
