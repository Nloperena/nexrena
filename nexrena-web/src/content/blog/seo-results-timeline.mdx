SEO compounds. It doesn't flip a switch. Here's what to expect.

## Technical Fixes (Weeks to Months)

- **Crawl errors, redirects, site speed** — Google re-crawls. Improvements can show in 2–8 weeks.
- **Schema, meta, structure** — Once indexed, impact can be faster. Rich results may take a few weeks.

## Content and Authority (3–6 Months)

- **New pages** — Need to be crawled, indexed, and evaluated. 4–12 weeks for new content to rank.
- **Topic clusters** — Building authority takes time. 3–6 months for meaningful movement.
- **Competitive terms** — If competitors have been at it for years, expect 6–12 months.

## What Accelerates Results

- **Low competition** — Long-tail, niche terms rank faster.
- **Technical foundation** — Fix crawl and speed first. Content performs better on a solid base.
- **Consistent execution** — Weekly or bi-weekly content. Compound effect.
