Discovery is the foundation. Skip it and you'll build the wrong thing. Do it right and design and development are informed by data.

## What We Audit

- **Analytics** — Traffic, conversion, behavior. Where do users go? Where do they drop?
- **Content** — Every page. What stays, what goes, what gets rewritten.
- **Technical** — Speed, SEO, accessibility. Baseline before we build.
- **Competitors** — What's working? What's missing? Context for differentiation.

## Stakeholder Interviews

- **Goals** — What does success look like? Lead volume, conversion, brand?
- **Buyers** — Who are they? What do they need at each stage?
- **Pain points** — What's broken today? What frustrates users?
- **Constraints** — Budget, timeline, compliance. What can't we do?

## Deliverables

- **Findings summary** — What we found. What we'll fix.
- **Content inventory** — Page-by-page. Keep, revise, remove.
- **Information architecture** — Proposed sitemap.
- **Success metrics** — How we'll measure. Baseline + targets.
