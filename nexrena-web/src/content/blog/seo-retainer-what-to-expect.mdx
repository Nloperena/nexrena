SEO retainers vary wildly. Here's what a transparent agency does — and what you should ask for.

## Month 1: Audit and Foundation

- **Technical audit** — Crawl, speed, structure. Prioritized fix list.
- **Keyword research** — What to target. Commercial and informational.
- **Content gap** — What's missing? What to create first?
- **90-day plan** — Week-by-week. What gets done, when.

## Ongoing: Execution

- **Technical fixes** — Implemented. Not just recommendations.
- **Content** — 2–4 pieces per month. Briefs, writing, or both.
- **Optimization** — On-page, internal linking. Ongoing improvement.
- **Reporting** — Traffic, rankings, leads. Monthly. Tied to your goals.

## What to Ask

- **What's included?** — Hours? Deliverables? Content? Be specific.
- **Who works on it?** — Dedicated or shared? Senior or junior?
- **How do you measure?** — Vanity metrics or pipeline? Tie to revenue.
