A content calendar keeps you consistent. Here's how to build one.

## Topic Clusters

- **Pillar** — Broad topic. "SEO for manufacturers." "B2B web design."
- **Cluster** — Supporting pieces. "Technical SEO audit." "Manufacturing website best practices."
- **Map** — Cluster → pillar → commercial. Every piece links to the next.

## Priority

- **Commercial intent** — "SEO for manufacturers" before "what is SEO." Commercial first.
- **Gap** — What competitors rank for that you don't? Fill gaps.
- **Quick wins** — Low competition, high intent. Target those first.

## Cadence

- **Weekly or bi-weekly** — Consistency compounds. Don't burn out with daily.
- **Quality over quantity** — One strong piece beats three weak ones.
- **Update** — Refresh old content. Add sections. Update stats. "Last updated" helps.

## Calendar

- **Topic** — What we're writing.
- **Keyword** — Primary target.
- **Due date** — When it publishes.
- **Links** — Internal links to add. From and to.
