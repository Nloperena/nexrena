Landing pages exist to convert. One offer, one CTA, minimal distraction. Here's what works for B2B.

## Headline and Value Prop

- **Clear benefit** — "Get the 2024 B2B Website Redesign Checklist." Not "Download our guide."
- **Specific** — "10-page technical SEO audit" beats "Free audit."
- **Above the fold** — User should understand the offer in 3 seconds.

## Form Design

- **Minimal fields** — Email, name, company. Add more only if the offer justifies it.
- **Single column** — Easy to scan. No cognitive overload.
- **Button copy** — "Get the checklist" not "Submit." Action-oriented.

## Trust Elements

- **Social proof** — "Join 500+ B2B marketers." If true.
- **Privacy** — "We won't spam you." "Unsubscribe anytime."
- **No clutter** — Remove nav, footer links. One path: convert or leave.
