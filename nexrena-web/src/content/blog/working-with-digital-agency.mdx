The first week sets the tone. Here's what a transparent agency does — and what they need from you.

## Day 1: Kickoff

- **Goals alignment** — What does success look like? Lead volume, conversion rate, organic traffic?
- **Scope confirmation** — What's in, what's out, what's phase 2?
- **Access and tools** — Analytics, CMS, hosting, domain. Get these ready before kickoff.

## Days 2–3: Discovery

- **Audit** — Current site performance, SEO baseline, conversion paths.
- **Content inventory** — What exists, what's worth keeping, what gets rewritten.
- **Stakeholder map** — Who approves what? Who provides content?

## Days 4–5: First Deliverables

- **Findings summary** — What we found, what we'll fix first.
- **Sprint plan** — Week-by-week for the next 4–6 weeks.
- **Blockers** — Anything we need from you to proceed.

No black boxes. You should see work in progress by the end of week 1.
