Forzabuilt is a B2B industrial adhesives manufacturer serving the Florida and Southeast construction market.
Their previous website was a legacy WordPress installation with no SEO foundation, sub-40 Lighthouse scores,
and a contact form that hadn't generated a qualified lead in over six months.

## The Problem

Mid-size manufacturers in industrial verticals are chronically underserved by digital agencies. The category
assumes "good enough" — slow sites, stock photography, and generic copy that could describe any competitor.
Forzabuilt needed infrastructure that reflected the precision of their product.

## The Approach

We rebuilt the site on Next.js with a headless CMS, implementing structured data for product categories,
a gated technical specification library for qualified buyers, and a full technical SEO foundation from day one.
Every page was designed to serve both the plant manager and the procurement lead — different entry points,
same destination: a discovery call.

## The Result

Within 90 days of launch, organic inbound lead volume increased by 140%. The site now consistently scores
96+ on Lighthouse across all pages. The sales team reports that inbound leads arrive more informed —
they've already reviewed specs before picking up the phone.
