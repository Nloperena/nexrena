Furniture Packages USA provides turnkey furniture procurement for property investors, real estate developers,
and vacation rental managers throughout Florida. Their buying process is complex — bulk orders, multiple
unit types, custom configurations — but their website treated every visitor like a single-unit furniture shopper.

## The Problem

The core audience — property management companies and real estate investors — were arriving at a site
that didn't speak to them. No bulk ordering context, no developer-specific case studies, no clear
differentiation from consumer furniture retail. The SEO baseline was minimal; they were invisible for
the high-intent B2B search terms that describe exactly what they do.

## The Approach

We rebuilt the site on WordPress with a custom procurement workflow, restructuring navigation around
buyer type (investor, developer, property manager) rather than product category. A complete SEO content
strategy targeted 40+ B2B procurement intent keywords. Analytics and tracking were rebuilt from scratch
in GA4 with a Looker Studio reporting dashboard delivered monthly.

## The Result

Organic search traffic grew 210% over six months. Inbound quote requests nearly doubled. Average session
duration increased by over three minutes, indicating that the right buyers were engaging with the right
content before making contact. The site now ranks on page one for core B2B procurement terms in Florida.
