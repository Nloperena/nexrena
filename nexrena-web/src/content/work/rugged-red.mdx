Rugged Red is a premium outdoor equipment brand known for its high-performance gear designed for extreme conditions.
With a rapidly growing customer base, they outgrew their previous e-commerce platform which struggled with 
seasonal spikes and failed to deliver a seamless mobile experience.

## The Problem

The brand had strong demand but a brittle storefront. High-intent traffic during seasonal campaigns was 
often met with slow page loads and a multi-step checkout process that created significant friction for mobile 
users. Scaling to meet demand meant moving away from monolithic architecture.

## The Approach

We replatformed the entire storefront to a headless commerce architecture using React and AWS. This decoupled 
the frontend from the backend, allowing for sub-second page transitions and a custom, streamlined checkout 
flow integrated with Stripe. We also introduced an edge-caching layer to handle traffic bursts without sacrificing 
performance.

## The Result

The transition to headless architecture resulted in a 31% increase in checkout completion. Bounce rates dropped 
by 47% as mobile users experienced a significantly faster and more intuitive shopping journey. The new 
infrastructure maintained 99.95% uptime throughout peak seasonal campaigns, providing a stable foundation 
for the brand's continued growth.
