VITO Fryfilter produces a patented frying oil filtration system sold to commercial kitchens globally.
Their existing Shopify store was generating traffic from multiple international markets but converting poorly —
particularly in European and Australian channels where trust signals and localized content were absent.

## The Problem

High-intent international traffic was arriving at a store built for domestic U.S. buyers. Currency, language
context, and trust infrastructure (certifications, case studies by market) were all missing. The checkout
experience on mobile was creating significant drop-off.

## The Approach

We restructured the Shopify architecture to support market-specific landing pages, implemented hreflang
correctly across all international variants, rebuilt the product pages around the commercial kitchen buyer
persona, and overhauled mobile checkout. ROI calculators showing oil savings per quarter were added as
interactive conversion tools.

## The Result

International revenue increased 85% year-over-year within the first two quarters post-launch. Conversion
rate across all markets lifted 38%, with the largest gains in EU markets where localized trust content
had the most impact. Average order value increased 22% as bundle recommendations reached the right buyers.
