# Delivery Playbook — From Signed Deal to Happy Client

## The Process (Every Project)

### Phase 0: Kickoff (Day 1–3)

- [ ] Signed service agreement received
- [ ] 50% deposit invoiced and paid
- [ ] Add client to CRM in Nexrena Ops
- [ ] Create project in Nexrena Ops
- [ ] Send kickoff questionnaire (see below)
- [ ] Schedule kickoff call (30 min)

**Kickoff Questionnaire** (send as email or form):
1. What 3 words describe how you want your brand to feel?
2. What are your top 3 business goals for the next 12 months?
3. Who are your top 3 competitors?
4. Who is your ideal customer? Describe them.
5. What do you like about your current website? What do you hate?
6. Do you have brand guidelines, logos, or assets to share?
7. Who is the single decision-maker for approvals?

### Phase 1: Strategy & Architecture (Week 1–2)

**Web Design projects:**
- Competitor analysis
- Sitemap and information architecture
- Wireframes (low-fidelity)
- Content plan (what goes on each page)
- Present to client, get sign-off

**SEO projects:**
- Technical audit (Screaming Frog, Search Console, PageSpeed)
- Keyword research and gap analysis
- Content architecture (topic clusters, pillar pages)
- Present findings + roadmap, get sign-off

**Deliverable:** Strategy document shared with client for approval before design begins.

### Phase 2: Design & Build (Week 3–6)

**Web Design:**
- High-fidelity mockups (homepage + 2 key pages)
- Client review → 1 round of revisions
- Development (Astro/Next.js, headless if needed)
- Content population
- Internal QA (speed, mobile, accessibility)

**SEO:**
- Technical fixes implemented
- On-page optimization (titles, metas, headers, internal links)
- Content production (blog posts, landing pages)
- Schema markup and structured data
- Link building outreach (if in scope)

**Communication rule:** Send a short progress update every Friday. 3 bullets: what we did, what's next, any blockers.

### Phase 3: Launch & QA (Week 7–8)

- [ ] Cross-browser testing (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge)
- [ ] Mobile testing (iOS Safari, Android Chrome)
- [ ] Speed test (target: 90+ Lighthouse on all pages)
- [ ] Forms tested and working
- [ ] Analytics and tracking verified
- [ ] 301 redirects mapped (if redesign)
- [ ] Client walkthrough call
- [ ] Client sign-off
- [ ] Go live
- [ ] Post-launch smoke test
- [ ] Final 50% invoice sent

### Phase 4: Handoff & Growth (Week 9+)

- [ ] Handoff documentation (logins, how to update content)
- [ ] 30-day post-launch check-in call
- [ ] Propose retainer for ongoing growth (SEO, content, optimization)

## Client Communication Rules

| Rule | Details |
|------|---------|
| Response time | Same business day for emails, within 2 hours for urgent |
| Update frequency | Weekly Friday update, no exceptions |
| Meeting cadence | Kickoff + weekly 15-min check-in (optional) + review milestones |
| Revision rounds | 2 included in project scope. Additional rounds billed hourly. |
| Single point of contact | One decision-maker on their side. No design-by-committee. |

## When Things Go Wrong

**Client ghosts on feedback:**
> "Hi [Name], just following up on the designs we sent [date]. We're holding the timeline for your feedback — once we hear back, we'll move straight into [next phase]. Let me know if you need more time."

If no response in 7 days, send a "project pause" notice. The clock stops. Resume when they re-engage.

**Scope creep:**
> "That's a great idea. It falls outside the current scope, but we can add it as a Phase 2 item. I'll send a quick addendum with the additional cost."

Never say no. Say "yes, and here's what it costs."

**Client unhappy with design:**
> "Thanks for the honest feedback. Can you point to 2–3 specific things that aren't working? And if you have examples of sites you do like, that helps us dial it in fast."

Always redirect vague complaints into specific, actionable feedback.

## Invoicing

- Use Nexrena Ops to create and track invoices
- **Projects:** 50/50 split (deposit + final)
- **Retainers:** Monthly, invoiced on the 1st, due on the 5th
- **Late payments:** Friendly reminder at 7 days, firm reminder at 14 days, work pauses at 30 days
- Always include payment terms in the service agreement

## Quality Checklist (Before Showing Anything to Client)

- [ ] Spell-checked all copy
- [ ] All links work (no broken links)
- [ ] Mobile layout checked
- [ ] Load time under 3 seconds
- [ ] Brand colors and fonts consistent
- [ ] Forms submit correctly
- [ ] No lorem ipsum left anywhere
- [ ] Favicon and OG images set
