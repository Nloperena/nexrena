# Weekly Operating Rhythm

Running a business without a rhythm means reactive chaos. This is the cadence.

## Daily (90 minutes)

| Block | Duration | Activity |
|-------|----------|----------|
| Prospect | 15 min | Find 5 new potential clients, add to CRM |
| Outreach | 30 min | Send 5 personalized messages (LinkedIn DM or email) |
| Follow-up | 15 min | Respond to all open conversations |
| LinkedIn | 15 min | Post content + comment on 5 ICP posts |
| Admin | 15 min | Check email, invoices, lead notifications |

Do this first thing in the morning before client work. Non-negotiable.

## Weekly

### Monday: Plan the Week
- [ ] Review pipeline in Nexrena Ops — what stage is each deal at?
- [ ] Check active project status — any blockers?
- [ ] Set 3 priorities for the week (1 sales, 1 delivery, 1 growth)
- [ ] Schedule discovery calls and client check-ins

### Wednesday: Mid-Week Check
- [ ] How many outreach messages sent so far this week? (Target: 15+)
- [ ] Any proposals outstanding? Follow up.
- [ ] Any client deliverables due? Are they on track?

### Friday: Close the Week
- [ ] Send client progress updates (see delivery-playbook.md)
- [ ] Update Nexrena Ops — move deals through pipeline, log hours
- [ ] Write and schedule next week's LinkedIn posts
- [ ] Quick win: What's one thing you can ship or finish before Monday?

## Monthly Business Review (1 hour, first Monday of month)

### Revenue Dashboard

| Metric | Track This |
|--------|-----------|
| Revenue collected | Total payments received this month |
| Revenue invoiced | Total invoiced (may differ from collected) |
| Accounts receivable | Who owes you money? How old? |
| Recurring revenue (MRR) | Sum of active retainers |
| Pipeline value | Total value of open proposals/deals |

### Sales Dashboard

| Metric | Track This | Target |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| Outreach volume | Messages sent | 100+/month |
| Discovery calls | Calls booked | 4–8/month |
| Proposals sent | Proposals delivered | 2–4/month |
| Close rate | Proposals won / sent | 30–50% |
| Average deal size | Revenue / deals closed | $15k+ |

### Delivery Dashboard

| Metric | Track This |
|--------|-----------|
| Active projects | How many in progress? |
| On-time delivery | Any projects behind schedule? |
| Client satisfaction | Any complaints or concerns? |
| Hours logged | Are you tracking time? (Even if not billing hourly — track for profitability) |

### Growth Dashboard

| Metric | Track This |
|--------|-----------|
| LinkedIn followers | Growing? |
| Website leads | How many contact form submissions? |
| Organic traffic | Is nexrena.com traffic growing? |
| Case studies published | Added any new proof this month? |
| Referrals received | Any this month? |

## Quarterly Strategy Review (2 hours)

Every 3 months, zoom out:

### Questions to Answer

1. **Revenue:** Are you on track for your annual target?
2. **Pipeline:** Is there enough work coming in for next quarter?
3. **Pricing:** Should you raise prices? (see financial-operations.md)
4. **Services:** Is one service significantly more profitable? Double down.
5. **Clients:** Which clients are most valuable? Which are draining?
6. **Content:** What's working on LinkedIn? What blog posts are driving traffic?
7. **Tools:** Are you paying for anything you don't use? Cancel it.
8. **Hiring:** Do you need contractor help? (see financial-operations.md)

### Quarterly Actions

- [ ] Update pricing if warranted
- [ ] Publish at least 1 new case study
- [ ] Refresh nexrena.com with any new work or testimonials
- [ ] Review and update the sales playbook based on what's actually closing
- [ ] Set 3 goals for next quarter (specific, measurable)

## Health Indicators

### Green (Healthy)
- 3+ months of operating expenses in reserve
- 2+ retainer clients active
- Pipeline has 2+ deals in proposal stage
- Closing 1+ new project per month
- Responding to leads within 24 hours

### Yellow (Attention Needed)
- Less than 2 months of operating expenses in reserve
- Only 1 retainer client
- Pipeline has fewer than 2 active deals
- Haven't closed a new project in 6+ weeks
- Outreach volume dropped below 50 messages/month

### Red (Take Action Now)
- Less than 1 month of expenses in reserve
- Zero retainer clients
- Empty pipeline — no active proposals
- No new project in 8+ weeks
- Not posting on LinkedIn or doing outreach

### If You Hit Red

1. **Stop everything else.** Sales is the only priority.
2. **Double outreach volume** — 10 messages/day instead of 5
3. **Reach out to past clients** — "Any projects coming up?"
4. **Drop pricing floor temporarily** — Take a $5k–$8k project to get cash flow moving
5. **Post on LinkedIn daily** — with result-focused content
6. **Consider freelance platforms** — Toptal, Upwork as a bridge, not a long-term play

The goal is never to hit red. The weekly rhythm prevents it.

## The Non-Negotiable Habits

These are the habits that, if you do nothing else, keep the business alive:

1. **Send 5 outreach messages every business day** — 100/month fills a pipeline
2. **Post on LinkedIn 3x per week** — compounds visibility
3. **Follow up on every lead within 24 hours** — speed wins deals
4. **Send Friday client updates** — prevents surprises and builds trust
5. **Invoice immediately** — don't let money sit on the table
6. **Review your numbers on the 1st of every month** — you can't improve what you don't measure
