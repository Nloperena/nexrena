## Who You Sell To

**Mid-size B2B companies** in three verticals:

- **Manufacturing** — Complex catalogs, technical buyers, long sales cycles
- **E-Commerce** — Category page SEO, headless performance, checkout optimization
- **Professional Services** — Law, consulting, accounting. Credibility + lead capture

**Ideal client profile:** $2M–$50M revenue, 10–200 employees, website is outdated or underperforming, founder or marketing lead is frustrated with current results.
