# Pricing Guide

## Packages

### Web Design & Development

| Tier | Scope | Price |
|------|-------|-------|
| Starter | 5–7 page site, modern design, mobile-first, CMS | $10,000–$15,000 |
| Growth | 10–20 pages, conversion architecture, custom animations, headless CMS | $15,000–$30,000 |
| Enterprise | Full rebuild, e-commerce or portal, custom integrations, multi-language | $30,000–$50,000+ |

**Timeline:** 6–12 weeks depending on tier.

### SEO & Search Growth

| Tier | Scope | Price |
|------|-------|-------|
| Audit Only | Technical audit + roadmap, one-time deliverable | $3,000–$5,000 |
| Growth | Audit + technical fixes + content (6-month engagement) | $10,000–$25,000 |
| Full Authority | Everything above + link building + ongoing content | $25,000–$50,000+ |

**Timeline:** SEO is a 6–12 month play. Set expectations early.

### Retainers (Full-Service Growth)

| Tier | Includes | Monthly |
|------|----------|---------|
| Essentials | Maintenance, analytics, 2 blog posts/mo, minor updates | $3,000/mo |
| Growth | Above + A/B testing, SEO optimization, quarterly roadmap | $5,000/mo |
| Strategic | Above + new feature development, paid media management | $8,000/mo |

**Minimum commitment:** 3 months. Discount at 6 or 12.

## How to Quote

1. **Listen first.** Understand their problem before talking price.
2. **Anchor high.** Start with the Growth tier. Only go to Starter if budget is genuinely tight.
3. **Bundle wins.** "Web + SEO together" is more valuable and stickier than either alone.
4. **Phase it.** If budget is a concern, break into Phase 1 (site) + Phase 2 (SEO) with separate invoices.
5. **Never discount.** Reduce scope instead. "We can do X in Phase 1 and Y in Phase 2."

## Payment Terms

| Type | Structure |
|------|-----------|
| Projects | 50% deposit, 50% on completion |
| Retainers | Monthly, invoiced 1st, due by 5th |
| Add-ons | Invoiced upon delivery |

## When to Walk Away

- Budget under $5,000 for a website (they need a freelancer, not Nexrena)
- "Can you just make it look like Apple?" with no strategic clarity
- Design-by-committee with 5+ stakeholders and no single decision-maker
- They want revisions forever with no approval process
- Red flags on payment (asking for Net 60+, reluctant to sign agreement)

Your time is your most limited resource. Protect it.
