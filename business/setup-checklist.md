# Professional Setup Checklist

Everything you need to look legitimate and be operational. Do these in order.

## Tier 1: Do Today (Before First Outreach)

### Google Business Profile
- [ ] Create at google.com/business
- [ ] Business name: **Nexrena**
- [ ] Category: "Web design agency" (primary), "SEO agency" (secondary)
- [ ] Address: Kissimmee, FL (use your address or set as service-area business)
- [ ] Hours: Mon–Fri, 9am–6pm ET
- [ ] Website: https://nexrena.com
- [ ] Description: "Nexrena is a digital growth agency serving mid-size companies. Web design, SEO, and full-service growth. Based in Kissimmee, FL (Orlando metro)."
- [ ] Add photos: Screenshot of nexrena.com homepage, brand images
- [ ] Request verification (usually a postcard or phone call)

### Email

## Tier 2: Do This Week

### Legal & Business
- [ ] LLC registered in Florida (if not already done)
- [ ] EIN obtained from IRS (irs.gov — free, instant)
- [ ] Business bank account opened (separate from personal)
- [ ] Contractor agreement template ready (for when you hire help)
- [ ] Service agreement template reviewed (in Founding Documents)

### Financial Setup
- [ ] Accounting tool set up (Wave is free, QuickBooks Self-Employed for $15/mo)
- [ ] Tax savings account opened (separate savings account, label it "Taxes")
- [ ] Stripe or payment method connected to Nexrena Ops invoicing
- [ ] First retainer and project invoice templates created in Ops

### Communication
- [ ] Slack or Discord workspace for client communication (optional, some prefer email)
- [ ] Loom account (free tier) — for recording quick video walkthroughs for clients
- [ ] Google Drive folder structure:
